Learning is a Journey

Custom made lessons for your particular style

by Erin Atwater

Whether you are five or fifty, whether you are polishing up skills from your youth or starting from scratch, Erin will customize the lesson plan to fit your needs and learning style.

Nervous around horses? Erin will take things slowly, building your skills up at your pace without the pressure to perform in front of a class.

Have a particular learning need? Erin will cater the lesson to your particular learning style without judgement or prejudice. And of course, the horses will, too!

Have your own horse that you'd like some training with? Erin can meet you at a location and create a custom plan for both you and your horse.
