Meet our horses!

Chili

Chili is an eleven-year-old arabian-rocky mountain gelding with a lot of patience and a lot of pride. Standing at 15 hands, he is the perfect beginner to intermediate level horse for all ages, and he knows it.

Iris

Iris is a pretty, 14.3 hand full arabian mare who is as loving as she is eager to please. At fourteen-years-old, her patience makes her particularly good with children. Happily in your pocket, she is an excellent horse for beginner to intermediate riders.

Phoebe

With a lot more go still in her than woah, this eighteen year old off the track mare is a smooth ride for intermediate to advanced riders. Standing at 16 hands tall, this retired race horse will trot with you for hours, and slobber all over you in thanks afterwards.

Dallas

Another retired racehorse, this gelding's old bones prevent him from carrying anything but lighter riders, but his smooth gait, his twenty-two years of experience, and his loving nature make him a joy for any intermediate rider. Standing at 15.3 hands, he is gentle and calm with even the littlest of children.

Callie

For our smallest learners, Callie is there to save the day! A pony who stands at 9 hands tall, she is plucky, loving, and eager to please. Whether it's being riden around the round pen or having a five year old learn to lunge, this plucky pony is ready to go!

Comet

At just over a year old, Comet is not quite ready to give lessons. Still, he has stolen our hearts. He is a spunky stallion about 9 hands tall that is learning the basics of halter training.
